AboutUs

Great South Autobody (GSA), is located in the south of Johannesburg and has been in service since 1972. Since inception our family business has been known for our friendly service and excellent workmanship. We're a level 2 BEE contributor and have been accredited with a 100% recognition level.


Click here for our BEE certificate.

We offer vehicle owners a complete repair package from advanced structural repair to professional paint refinishing. By using the latest technology and an excellent calibre of staff, we provide paint work with a lifetime guarantee. It would be expected that due to our state of the art equipment and expert workmanship that our pricing would be inflated, however, our clients will pleased to know that our prices are quoted against industry standards.
